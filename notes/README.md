# Notes

This directory contains the research notes that accompany my work in nanophotonics, optical spectroscopy, and theoretical physics.

Rather than polished publications, these documents are **living notes** developed throughout my research. They include derivations, literature summaries, technical references, experimental protocols, and conceptual explanations that support both ongoing projects and published work.

Most notes are written in LaTeX and are continuously expanded as my understanding evolves. Hopefully, they will serve as start point to study for young researchers.

## Contents

### Spectroscopy

Working notes on experimental and theoretical spectroscopy, including topics such as:

- Raman spectroscopy
- Fourier and angle-resolved spectroscopy
- Pump–probe spectroscopy
- Optical characterization techniques
- Data analysis and calibration

## Future topics

As the repository grows, additional notes will cover areas including:

- Strong light–matter interaction
- Quantum optics
- Computational physics
- Scientific programming
- Mathematical methods for physics
